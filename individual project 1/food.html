Resturants

Pacci's trattoria

The restaurant offers a variety of classic Italian dishes, including pasta, pizza, and seafood. The ambiance is cozy and intimate, with dim lighting and rustic decor. Pacci's Trattoria is known for its friendly service and delicious food, making it a popular spot for locals and visitors alike.

6 Post Office Rd, Silver Spring, MD 20910

(301) 588-1011

Sushi Jin

Sushi Jin is a Japanese restaurant located in downtown Silver Spring. It offers a variety of sushi rolls, sashimi, and other Japanese dishes. The restaurant has a modern and sleek interior with a sushi bar and a dining area. The menu features a range of traditional and contemporary sushi options, as well as appetizers, soups, salads, and entrees.

8555 Fenton St, Silver Spring, MD 20910

(301) 608-0990

Cubano's

Located in the heart of downtown Silver Spring, lies Cubanos; a dining establishment that serves authentic Cuban cuisine. With an array of traditional dishes such as empanadas, ropa vieja and Cuban sandwiches, it's no surprise why this restaurant is highly sought after by many food enthusiasts.Their atmosphere exudes a relaxed vibe with bright decor accents scattered throughout their premises while welcoming servers deliver top-notch service to each patron they attend to.

1201 Fidler Ln, Silver Spring, MD 20910

(301) 563-4020


Jerk House Eatery Beer and Wine

Jerk House Eatery Beer and Wine is located in the Four Corners Shopping Center that serves authentic Jamaican cuisine. With an extensive menu and tons of options you will definetly get a taste of Jamaica at this restaurant. Be weary though, this spot doesn't have much seating, so planning ahead or getting takeout might be your best bet.

25 University Blvd W, Silver Spring, MD 20901

(301) 681-0070
