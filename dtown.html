Downtown Silver Spring

Downtown Silver Spring is located in the heart of Maryland, just outside Washington. It's known for its vibrant arts and entertainment scenes, diverse cuisine, and a wide range of places to shop. Downtown is also home to the Discovery Headquaters and the American Film Institute. In additon, there are a variety of public spaces such as the Silver Spring Civic Building and Veterans Plaza. This is one of the most popular places in all of Silver Spring. There is just so much to do.


The Filmore

The Filmore is a live music venue that has hosted a wide range of artists since its opening in 2011 .It has a capacity of 2000 people with state of the art lighting and sound systems. While this is small a small space, the close proximity to the performers makes the expericence unique. If you can catch a performance while in Silver Spring, it will be a experience to remember!


American Film Institute

The American Film Institute and Culture Center is historic movie theatre in Downtown Silver Spring The theatre showcases a range of films from independent to classic and foreingn. AFI also hosts Film festivals and special events throughout the year. Aside from movies, the AFI has a cafe, bookstore, and and art gallery.


Shopping

Downtown Silver Spring offers a variety of shopping options, ranging from major retails to boutique shops. There is a large in door Mall - City Place Mall which has major retail stores like Macy's, Marshalls, H&M, and Michaels. The surrounding areas have a mix of chain Resturants ,clothing, bookstores, and electronic stores. Additonally the Fenton Street Market which is a weekly outdoor market that offers unique handmade items and food from locals.
